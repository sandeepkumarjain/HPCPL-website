### 7. Products Section

**Section Title**  
Engineered Product Range for Diverse Road and Infrastructure Applications

**Section Intro**  
We supply a broad range of bitumen grades and specialty products backed by technical documentation, quality reports, and responsive commercial support.

**Product Grid**

**VG 10 Bitumen**  
Suitable for cold climate paving, surface dressing, and spray works where lower viscosity supports smoother application.

**VG 30 Bitumen**  
The preferred grade for highways, asphalt roads, and general paving applications requiring a dependable balance of stiffness and workability.

**VG 40 Bitumen**  
Designed for heavy traffic corridors and high-temperature conditions where stronger resistance is required.

**CRMB**  
Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen developed for enhanced flexibility, durability, and longer road life under demanding conditions.

**PMB**  
Polymer Modified Bitumen for premium highway applications requiring improved performance, stability, and fatigue resistance.

**Emulsion Grades**  
RS-1, RS-2, MS, and SS grades suitable for surface treatment, tack coat, patchwork, and maintenance applications.

**Imported Bitumen**  
Bulk import supply solutions supported by sourcing expertise, port coordination, and structured inland movement.

## 5. Premium UX Recommendations

- Use a dark industrial palette with copper, steel, and warm amber accents.
- Pair a strong serif or display font for headings with a clean sans-serif for body text.
- Use video, gradients, and subtle industrial textures instead of flat backgrounds.
- Animate counters, charts, and section reveals with restraint.
- Highlight trust, operational scale, and movement rather than generic corporate visuals.
- Keep every major section connected to a conversion action.
